Project Description

This Future of Heat project pertains to educating children and the general public on the now emerging push towards the electrification of home heating. The conventional method for home heating, mainly utilizes furnaces, which burn fossil fuels, like natural gas, for heat. As the need to reduce carbon emissions grows ever more imminent, certain cities internationally have taken steps towards banning fossil fuel based heating to usher in the transition towards electrical heating. As such, it is important to understand the effects of this transition towards electrical heating.
